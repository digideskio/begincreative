What's the problem?

Having a web presence at launch is standard for every start-up. Most new start-ups revolve entirely around the web and technology. We're here to help you get set up with what you need without charging an arm and a leg like a traditional design agency.

What do we offer?

We're a team of students from up and down the UK with plenty of expertise. We create brands, websites, mobile applications and digital marketing. Just because we're students, doesn't mean we can't launch succesful products. Take a look for yourself.

How do we make a living?

We're designed for start-ups - that's why we're flexible. Grab a coffee with us and if we like your idea we'll work for a small amount of equity (%) in your idea. If that doesn't work for you then you can always just buy us a few beers. We are students after all.

FAQ

Haven't named your idea yet?

Don't worry, we're good at coming up with great name ideas. We promise it'll be a little more original than our own name.

Don't have a logo?

We've got a team of graphic designers at your disposal with a tone of branding experience. You'll have your very own Nike Swoosh in no time.

Still haven't got a site?

Our team primarily consists of web designers and developers so you don't need to worry about getting your 2.0 looking website.

Need a mobile app?

We've got a few geeks in house who know how to make native Android and iOS apps. We're also all experienced with HTML5 and CSS3, so PhoneGap apps are on the cards too.

What about something fancy?

We can put together pretty complex web applications too. We use PHP, MySQL and occasionally ASP.NET. It's all scalable with Amazon Web Services or Microsoft Azure when it's time to deploy the app.
